## ⚠️ The Problem in the Fitness Industry
Most TDEE calculators and fitness apps fail the average Indian woman because:
1. **Unrealistic Diets:** They output generic macro numbers (e.g., "Eat 130g protein") without considering local Indian grocery budgets or food availability.
2. **Biological Ignorance:** They ignore critical female physiological needs like bone density loss, iron deficiency (anemia), and hormonal imbalances (PCOS/Menstrual health).
3. **Dangerous Workouts:** They prescribe advanced, high-impact exercises (like barbell back squats or 6-day splits) to beginners, leading to joint pain and injuries.
4. **Data Harvesting:** They require sign-ups and harvest personal physical data.

## 🚀 The SagarXFit Solution
The **Women's Rational Blueprint** is not just a calculator; it's an automated digital health coach. It operates entirely on client-side JavaScript, ensuring 100% privacy, and uses a custom "Greedy Algorithm" to build mathematically perfect, budget-friendly Indian diet stacks.

### Core Engineering & Scientific Features:

* **🧠 The Biological Math Engine:** Utilizes the strict **Mifflin-St Jeor Equation** for BMR. It features a built-in safety floor (caps the deficit at 1200 kcal) to permanently prevent metabolic damage from crash diets.
* **🛒 The "Greedy" Grocery Matrix:** A dynamic cart loop that fetches the cheapest, high-quality Indian protein sources (Soya, Whole Eggs, Moong Dal, Tofu) based on the user's exact budget (e.g., ₹100/day), while locking in trace protein from typical "Home Food" (Roti/Dal).
* **🩸 Hormonal & Blood Health Add-ons:** Hardcoded medical/nutritional guidance section prescribing Iron (Kala Chana/Spinach + Vitamin C), Omega-3 Lignans (Flaxseeds for estrogen balance), and Calcium (Curd/Ragi) to fight standard Indian dietary deficiencies.
* **🏋️‍♀️ Biomechanical Safety Protocol:** Age- and experience-adjusted exercise prescriptions. Replaces spine-loading movements with joint-safe alternatives (Goblet Squats, Glute Bridges) to fix posture issues caused by desk jobs. Includes a strict 48-hour recovery warning.
* **📸 1-Click Viral Export:** Integrated with `html2canvas` to instantly render the user's custom blueprint into a high-definition, shareable image for their phone gallery.
* **🌍 True Accessibility:** Features a built-in [EN | A-अ] Hinglish language toggle for maximum reach across the Indian demographic. Offline-capable (PWA).

## 🛠 Tech Stack
* **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3 (Custom Dark Theme with Rose Gold accents).
* **Logic:** Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+).
* **Libraries:** `html2canvas` (for UI-to-Image generation).
* **Architecture:** Progressive Web App (PWA) with Service Worker for offline functionality and zero-backend data privacy.
